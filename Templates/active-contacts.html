Report Description

The Active Contacts Report is used to create custom lists of grouped contacts. It's main purpose is to compile lists of contacts for mass communication to certain groups within CTP. This report uses all CTP contacts including Clients, Providers, and Office Staff, both active and inactive.
Some Central Reach labels may be inaccurate due to duplicates in their system.
Support = Office Staff Label
Non-Provider and Pre-Employment labels are depricated.
