Report Description

The Late Conversions report is used for billing to find any appointments converted after their respective pay period. It can be used to find any appointments converted later than the chosen Converted After date as well, even if the date range selected does not coincide with a pay period. It finds all appointments withing the selected date range that are converted after the selected Converted After date.
Some Central Reach labels may be inaccurate due to duplicates in their system.
This report does not include unconverted appointments.
