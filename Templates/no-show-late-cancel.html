Report Description

The No Show/Late Cancellation Report is for billing purposes. It details all cancelled appointments that were either No Show or Late Cancellations (less than 2 hours notice). This can be general for all appointments in the given date range or it can be for a specific school, client, or provider. Any combination of filters work as well.
Some Central Reach labels may be inaccurate due to duplicates in their system.
