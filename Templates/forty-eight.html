Report Description

The 48 Hour Late Conversion Report looks at all appointments and conversions between the given dates. It then determines if those appointments were converted late based on our 48 hour conversion policy. If any appointment is late or is still unconverted, this report will show it. The lists generated on this page may be ignored. They pertain to auto email functionality.
Some Central Reach labels may be inaccurate due to duplicates in their system.
This report is based on the 48 hour rule only and will show any appointment that breaks our 48 hour rule.
This report will have auto email functionality, but it does not send emails, currently.
