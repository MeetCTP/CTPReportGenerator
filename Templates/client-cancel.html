Report Description

The High Client Cancellation Report will show all cancelled appointments of the selected types within the selected date range. It then groups all sessions by provider/client pairings and calculates if each provider/client pair has had 3 cancallations in a row or not. It also calculates the percentage of sessions that were cancelled. All these calculations are broken down month to month. This report will also be ordered chronologically.
Some Central Reach labels may be inaccurate due to duplicates in their system.
